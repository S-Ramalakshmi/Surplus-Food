How Surplus Share Works

A simple process connecting food donors with communities in need

1
Report Surplus Food
Businesses report available surplus food through our simple online form or mobile app.
2
We Verify & Match
Our system verifies food safety and matches with nearby NGOs based on current needs.
3
Quick Pickup
Trained volunteers from partner NGOs coordinate quick, safe pickup of the food.
4
Distribution
Food is distributed to people in need through schools, shelters, and community centers.
